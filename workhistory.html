Guidelines for completing your Work History

You will be prompted to enter the details of all work performed over the last 18 months, starting with your employment on or after 01/13/2024 and onwards. Use the following guidelines for each employment:


  • US Military - Active Duty
  • US Military - Civilian Duty
  • Other Federal Government Agency



  • You worked as a regular part-time or full-time employee
  • You worked as an individual contractor
  • You performed work as a gig economy worker (app-based employment)

If the answer is 'Yes', please follow the steps below to identify your employer for this employment.

Step 1. Search for/select the employer for whom you performed this work.

Step 2. If you are unable to find the employer you are searching for, you are encouraged to review your search criteria and try again so as to not delay the processing of your claim.
Step 3. If you are sure that your employer cannot be found via the 'Search' feature, you should proceed to manually enter the details of your employer.

If the answer is 'No', you will be considered to be self-employed for the purposes of unemployment benefits. Please follow the steps below to identify your self-employment business.

Step 1. Search for/select your business

Step 2. If you are sure that your business cannot be found via the 'Search' feature, you should proceed to manually enter the details of your business.
